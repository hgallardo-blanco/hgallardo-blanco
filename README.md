## Hi there 👋 I'm Hugo Leonid Gallardo-Blanco

**Researcher in Human & Molecular Genetics · Targeted Nanomedicine**
School of Medicine, Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León (UANL) — Oncology Service, Hospital Universitario "Dr. José Eleuterio González", Monterrey, México.
National Researcher (SNII, Level 1).

I work at the interface of **cancer biology, genetics and nanomedicine**, building both wet-lab strategies and open, reproducible computational tools.

### 🔬 Research lines
- **HER2+ targeted nanomedicine** — aptamer-coated AuNPs / polymeric NPs delivering mRNAs, miRNAs, antimiRs and peptides.
- **MYC/MAX oncogenic program** — pMyc/pMax peptide constructs (prostate cancer).
- **In vitro transcription (IVT) mRNA platform** — T7, CleanCap, poly(A).
- **Post-GWAS functional annotation** — reproducible pipelines (T2D / metabolic loci).
- **Breast cancer genetics in Northeastern Mexico** — gene–environment interactions; ACMG/CAP variant interpretation.

### 🧰 Open tools & resources (Gallardo BioTools)
- **StriderWeb** — nucleic-acid sequence analysis · DOI:10.5281/zenodo.20725635
- **PrimerDesigner** — PCR primer design (NN Tm, in-silico PCR, Sanger) · DOI:10.5281/zenodo.20725380
- **Gallardo BioTools** (collection) · DOI:10.5281/zenodo.20723202 · https://gallardo-lab.github.io/gallardo-biotools/
